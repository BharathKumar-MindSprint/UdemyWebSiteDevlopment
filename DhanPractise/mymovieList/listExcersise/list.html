Bharath Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup of warm water
  • 2 1/4 teapspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling:

  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened

Instruction

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and to the mixture.
  3. Add in the Flour and mix untill into dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes.
  5. Transfer te dought for large bowl and cover with plastic wrap.Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
  6. After 2 hours,roll out the dough into a rectangle.
  7. Spread the softened butter over the dough.
