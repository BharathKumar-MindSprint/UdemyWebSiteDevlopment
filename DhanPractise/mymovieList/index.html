The Best Movies According to Bharath

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Thuppaki

Thuppaki is a 2012 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by A. R. Murugadoss, featuring Vijay and Kajal Aggarwal in lead roles.

Fast And Furious

Fast and Furious is the best Film. I love the film with Tokyo Drift.

Dhangal

Dangal is a 2016 Indian Hindi-language biographical sports drama film directed by Nitesh Tiwari, based on the life of wrestler Mahavir Singh Phogat.
